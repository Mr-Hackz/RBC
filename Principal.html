Principal's Welcome!



We welcome all our students
and their families to Riverbanks College B-12

Welcome to our website. I am privileged to be the foundation Principal of Riverbanks College B-12, a brand-new state of the art school in the heart of Angle Vale. Offering a seamless education experience for children from Birth to Year 12.

We are very fortunate to have world-class, purpose-built facilities that will support and enhance teaching and learning.

Through world-class teaching and learning practices we will create a vibrant and inclusive B-12 educational community that fosters kind and resilient life-long learners, who will thrive in today and tomorrow’s world.

We are committed to ensuring that Riverbanks College B-12 is a place of belonging, where every child matters and every child thrives.

We have recruited highly skilled and passionate members of our leadership, teaching and paraprofessional teams, all of whom are kind, hard-working and relationship driven.

We will be relentless in our pursuit of equity and excellence and we look forward to working in close partnership with our families and our community to ensure happiness and success for all our children.

Please take the time to explore our site to discover what makes Riverbanks College B-12 such a special place to learn, connect and grow.

If you have questions that are not yet answered by the website, then you are warmly invited to contact the school by email at dl.1913.info@schools.sa.edu.au

Joe Priolo
Pincipal
